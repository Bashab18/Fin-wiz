Lesson Overview

In this tutorial, we'll cover the fundamentals of logging in securely and managing your passwords effectively. You'll learn how to create strong passwords, use password managers, and protect your accounts from unauthorized access.

Key Points

?? Strong Passwords

Use at least 12 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols

?? Unique Passwords

Never reuse passwords across different accounts

?? Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA whenever possible for extra security

?? Password Managers

Use a password manager to store and generate secure passwords
